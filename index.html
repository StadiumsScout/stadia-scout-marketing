Privacy

Your data, your control.

We collect only what's needed to make Stadia Scout work. Here's exactly what that means.

What data do we collect?

We collect your email address, display name, and handle when you create an account. When you log a stadium visit, we store your ratings, notes, photos, and the stadium visited. We do not collect location data in the background or sell your information to third parties.

How is my data used?

Your data is used solely to power the Stadia Scout experience — showing your passport, feed, badges, and visit history. Aggregated, anonymous data (like overall stadium ratings) is used to power the Rankings feature.

Who can see my visits?

By default, your visits are public and visible on your profile and in the social feed. You can mark individual visits as private at any time, and they will be hidden from all other users.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen in the app. Deletion is permanent and immediate.

Do you use cookies?

We use minimal cookies required for authentication (keeping you logged in). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

How do I contact you about privacy?

Email us at stadiscout@gmail.com with any privacy questions or data requests.
